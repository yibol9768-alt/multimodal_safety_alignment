\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

We identified a structural safety gap in action-token VLAs---base backbone and BC-trained action vocabulary decouple action generation from the natural-language refusal axis---and proposed \method{}+, a training-free hook that transplants a refusal direction from an externally-aligned sibling LLM. Direction-source control and a $\sqrt{k}$-normalized rank-$k$ sweep confirm the transplanted \emph{geometry} (not magnitude) is the active ingredient, living in a small ($k{=}5$, $+1.1$\,pp) multidimensional subspace. \method{}+ is a step toward treating text alignment as a \emph{reusable asset} across non-textual output pathways.
